Silver Spring, Maryland

BYOB & Corkage Restaurants in Silver Spring

If Bethesda is where you splurge, Silver Spring is where you bring your bottle without thinking twice — corkage here starts at just $10.

Silver Spring is the DMV's best-value pocket for BYOB. The fees are gentle, the cuisines run from Thai to Creole to Italian, and you can open a nice bottle on a weeknight without the special-occasion price tag. Below are the spots we've verified, sorted by corkage fee. As always, call ahead to confirm, and don't bring a wine that's already on their list.

Silver Spring Restaurants That Welcome Your Wine

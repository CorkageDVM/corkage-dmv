The BYOB Kit

What to Bring When You BYOB

Carrying a special bottle to dinner deserves a little preparation. These are the few things that keep your wine safe in transit, served at its best, and finished gracefully.

You've found a restaurant that welcomes your wine and chosen a bottle worth the occasion. A handful of small tools make the difference between simply transporting a bottle and arriving like you've done this before — and they make thoughtful gifts for the wine lover in your life.
